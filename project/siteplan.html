Website Planning Document

Site Name

Medical Care

This name was chosen because the website focuses on creating and managing digital medical records in a simple and accessible way.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide a digital platform where users can store, update, and manage their medical history. It will allow patients to track illnesses, treatments, and important health information in one place.

Scenarios

• How can I register my medical history online?

• How can I update my health information or add new medical conditions?

Color Scheme

Yellow (#FACC15): Used for highlights and accents

White: Used as the background color
